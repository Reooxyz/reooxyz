Once services have started, payments made to reoo.xyz are considered non-refundable. This helps us ensure efficient resource allocation and maintain the quality of our services.

Refunds will not be issued in the following scenarios:

  • The client engages in illegal, unethical, or non-compliant use of generated leads.

However, if you face any concerns during your campaign, we encourage you to contact us. We’ll do our best to assist you in optimizing your results, though refunds may not be applicable in such cases.

If you believe you are eligible for a refund, please email us at reoo.xyz@proton.me within 3 business days of the issue arising. Kindly include:
